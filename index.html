About Me

Hello, I'm Lara, an aspiring full stack developer hailing from the enchanting terrains of Portugal. My fascination with the transformative power of technology prompted me to delve into the captivating world of Python, which soon broadened to encompass a spectrum of coding languages. This self-directed journey has nurtured not only my technical skills, but also a deep-seated passion for my craft. My background in customer service and sales has allowed me to hone critical skills such as problem-solving, teamwork, and communication - skills I view as invaluable for effective software development.

  • Python (incl. OOP)
  • HTML/CSS
  • JavaScript
  • RESTful APIs
  • JSON
  • Tools: Pycharm, VS Code, Chrome Dev Tools, Github, Replit

As I transition further into the tech industry, I'm eager to blend these complementary skill sets to create meaningful and user-centered solutions. My unique background, coupled with my unwavering enthusiasm for coding, positions me uniquely in the realm of full stack development. I look forward to contributing to a team that values innovation and growth, and am excited for what the future holds as I continue my journey into tech!
